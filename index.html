Decoder Contact Cards Interactive personal branding contact cards. Using state-of-the-art decoder technology, these cards make finding their information a fun, interactive experience, in which two card holders must find each other to reveal the contact info. "If you value this information, find a like-minded friend to help you uncover the secrets within." The creation process involved painting my wife's hand white and photographing it in our kitchen using a sheet of paper as a backdrop and some can lights for our lighting rig. The cards were created with a sheet of red lighting gel sandwiched between four layers of card stock. 2017 American Advertising Awards Student Silver Award
